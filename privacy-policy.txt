PRIVACY POLICY
=============

Effective Date: February 8, 2026

StoryCast ("the App") is built to work locally on your device. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle.

NO APP DATA COLLECTION
----------------------

We do not collect, store, or sell personal data from the app.

The app does not include:
- Analytics or telemetry SDKs
- Advertising SDKs or ad identifiers
- Tracking across apps or websites
- Developer-run user profiling
- Developer-run remote logging of your activity

ON-DEVICE DATA STORAGE
----------------------

The following data is stored locally on your device for app functionality:
- Audiobook files you import
- Playback progress
- Chapters, folders, and library organization
- App preferences and settings

This data is managed with local iOS storage (file system and SwiftData).

NETWORK AND EXTERNAL SERVICES
-----------------------------

Core playback and library features work offline.

Some optional features can communicate outside the device only when you use them:
- In-app tips use Apple StoreKit for purchases
- Importing from iCloud Drive or another cloud file provider may download files to your device
- Contact Support opens your email app and sends a message only if you choose to send it
- AirPlay streams audio to the receiver you select (typically on your local network)

These are user-initiated system/service flows. They are not app analytics collection.

YOUR CONTROL
------------

You control your data in the app:
- Delete audiobooks and related metadata at any time
- Uninstall the app to remove app data from the device
- Use the app without creating an account

APP STORE PRIVACY LABELS
------------------------

In App Store Connect, this app is declared as Data Not Collected.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
------------------

Because the app does not collect personal data for developer analytics or profiling, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
----------------------

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The latest version will be published with the app documentation.
