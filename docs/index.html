Audiobook Player - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 29, 2026

Audiobook Player ("the App") is a privacy-focused audio player application developed with a commitment to user privacy. This policy explains our data practices.

NO DATA COLLECTION

We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data. The App operates entirely on your device with zero data collection.

What We Don't Collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, address)
  • Device identifiers (UDID, advertising ID)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Audio content metadata
  • Network data or connection information
  • Contact information
  • Payment information

DATA STORAGE ON DEVICE

All data associated with the App remains on your device:

  • Audiobook files (imported by you)
  • Playback progress and settings
  • Library organization (folders, bookmarks)
  • App preferences

This data is stored locally using iOS's standard storage mechanisms (File System and SwiftData) and is never transmitted externally.

NO NETWORK CONNECTIVITY

The App does not require internet access for its core functionality. The only network features are:

  • Importing audiobook files from local storage (File Importer)
  • AirPlay audio output (user-controlled device selection)
  • Background audio playback (system feature)

These features do not transmit any data to external servers.

YOUR CONTROL OVER DATA

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete Files: Remove audiobooks at any time via the App's interface
  • Clear All Data: Uninstalling the App removes all associated data
  • No Accounts: The App does not require user accounts or registration

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect data.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Since we collect no data, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is safe for all ages as it operates entirely locally.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy as the App evolves. Any changes will be reflected in updated documentation.

ACKNOWLEDGMENT

By using the Audiobook Player App, you acknowledge that the App operates entirely on your device with no data collection or transmission. We believe in privacy by design and default.

This privacy policy reflects our commitment to building software that respects user privacy while providing a seamless audiobook experience.
